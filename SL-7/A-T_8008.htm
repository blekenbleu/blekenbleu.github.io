    Audio-Technica AT331LP phono cartridge,
    also known as 8008 or 8008A Studio Reference cartridge

    Audio-Technica's finest and best P-mount T4P phono cartridge.  It is best known for its exquisite musicality, outstanding musical value, and high technology.  For these very reasons,
    Consumer Guide chose the Audio-Technica AT331LP as their recommended phono cartridge.
    The Audio-Technica AT331LP phono cartridge uses a Line Contact stylus to very accurately extract record groove information with a minimum of wear.  Audio-Technica used a tapered alloy tube cantilever (the hollow rod that extends from the cartridge to the diamond tip), for low mass and low resonance.  The Audio-Technica AT331LP phono catridge reproduces an extremely wide, extended frequency response with low distortion.
    It offers superlative tracking at 1 - 1.5 grams, saving your LPs from undue wear.

    Audio-Technica AT331LP Specifications

  • Output voltage at 1000 Hz, 5cm/sec.: 5.0 mV
  • Channel balance: 1.00 dB
  • Channel separation at 1 kHz/10kHz: 31/21 dB
  • Frequency response: 10-30,000 Hz
  • Stylus type: Linear Contact
  • Stylus construction: Nude round shank
  • Cantilever construction: Tapered alloy tube
  • Tracking force range: 1.00-1.50 g
  • Recommended load resistance: 47 kOhm
  • Cartridge color, body/stylus: Black/Red
  • Cartridge mount: T4P P-mount

    Extracted from the vinylengine Cartridge Database
    A fantastic tracking cartridge that sold for $395. This was Audio-Technica's last attempt to make an alloy cantilever with a combination of beryllium and boron with a titanium shaft. It has a frequency response of an amazing 5-30,000Hz.
    It has a Linear contact diamond that was an advanced Shibata design.  A fabulous cartridge!
