    Antec controller poorly matches Arctic case fans,
    which run fast at very low PWM % needed for quiet low load operation.
    Replacing puny stock Antec fan with Scythe's KazeFlex significantly altered airflow;
    most heat now flows out PC rear instead of top, with less noise.
    Case air pressure may now be negative,
    wanting top exhaust fans driven separately from front intake fans.
