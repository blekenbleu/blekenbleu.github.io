    9 Jul 2024  Initialize

    • Plug in ethernet and power, turn on
    • Sonic pad immediately downloaded 2 new firmwares
    • Selected Ender-3 V3 SE and wrote new firmware to microSDHC;
        did NOT flash the printer...
    • Sonic obviously used ethernet to download new code,
      but would not admit IP address
    • connected via Wi-Fi as 192.168.1.120:8819
    • problematic instructions
      • since not instructed otherwise, left original panel attached
        when inserting SDHC card for new firmware
      • Instructions directed USB-C; not really for Ender-3 V3 SE...
      • Original SE panel beeped horribly.. further instructions failed
      • tried again with another SDHC; same results
      • tried with panel removed, misfingered touch screen
      • tried again, plugging serial cable to Sonic,
        then unplugging and replugging..
      • communication established, whew!
    • started calibration.. I loathe that touch screen.
    • After printer setup, Wired Network is reported 192.168.1.40
      attempting to disable Wi-Fi still reports connected, wtf...
    • Reviewing bed heightmap on PC shows warp
      with upper left highest, upper right lowest,
        then fron left and right roughly equal.
    • IMO, web UI is hot garbage. Cannot find printer controls.
