Shipped in a proper objective jar, packaged in a bubble-lined envelope.
The objective appears to be in a single solid metal casting
and appears undamaged, but cannot focus in my AO 120 infinity microscope.
FWIW, also cannot focus in (finite) Optiphot; return requested.
Front optic is recessed about 1mm into the casting.

vendor offered $15 refund Jul 14
I tested further by holding it backward for far distant view (nearly infinite).
Good infinity objectives can focus a far image on ground glass
at nearly their working distance, but this cannot focus at any distance.
It nearly focuses when touching ground glass, so negative working distance?
Optical elements may be shifted inside, but I sense no looseness.
