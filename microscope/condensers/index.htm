Phase Contrast-2 ELWD 0.3 hack

 The intent was to repurpose this ELWD condenser as a darkfield turret.
The turret arrived before the lens.
Had I more carefully read that it was for inverted Diaphots only,
I would have been better prepared for disappointment.

The Phase Contrast-2 ELWD turret interferes with arched supports in -66 stage casting.
It does however barely fit under an Optiphot-1 stage. The -66 condenser rack pinion cannot be removed from the substage casting
without a special tool to engage 4 notches in the pinion bearing retainer.
Unlike that condensor rack, the substage rack seemingly cannot be removed,
even after removing the stop screw,
by simply continuing to raise or lower the coarse focus.
