Nikon stage bearings

The Metaphot was delivered with its stage partially disassembled and missing a roller bearing rail.
The good news: that missing rail is seemingly the same as Labophot and Optiphot x-travel stage rails,
and an eBay seller lists those racks with rails. Since I have a stage with stripped thread in its rail,
this provides working replacements for both stages.

Nikon Microscope Mechanical Stage X-rack for Labophot and Optiphot Series

eBay item number:  281567730299
Condition: Used

Nikon x-travel mechanical stage part that includes a good rack, good roller bearing rails and good cosmetic verniers.
If you have a stage mechanism that is damaged on a Nikon Labophot, Labophot-2. Optiphot or Optiphot-2
this is an easy fix. Even a novice can replace this unit without much trouble. The sale is for one each as pictured.

By now, I have enough experience to be aware that anytime something is described as "easy",
then it will be nearly impossible and almost certainly not worth the hassle, particularly for software.
