VR1 manufacturing proceeds in stages of different durations,
  not a continuous assembly line:
  • incoming parts inspection
  • eyeboxes: longest and most complex stage,
    with and without eye tracking,
  • device component preassemblies, e.g. frames, boards
  • final assembly
  • QA
  • boxing and shipment
This last 1.5 weeks finished certain amount of eyeboxes
- much better yield and assembly rate than before, quite a lot, still not to plan.
Next week: board preassembly, then final.
First assembled devices being ready to ship by end of September.

Eyebox assembly speed and yield are both improving.
