      and one additional identical cable (connected on the other side of the headset) to carry on the MR camera feed back to the PC

    • eye tracking is also their own implementation, in their framework, but will opensource everything about it, including the algorithm
    • motion compensation is in the framework they developed, and they will expose it for the sim community
    • they also mentioned about how the Mixed Reality cockpit mapping will work:
      you take the CAD of your car/airplane cockpit and feed it to their software,
      and then you can just interact with it with your hands (you'll need both hand tracking module and MR cameras on the HMD)
    • very large MR camera resolution, something like 4000 x 3000 I think
    • MR camera refresh rate is 90 fps
    • MR angle of view through cameras is 120, just like through the HMD,
      so you will not see the FoV shrink when you enable the MR camera passthrough
    • SteamVR needed for tracking

    • Sept update and sign-ups likely to slip into first week of Oct.

    • 3D video capture from passthru cameras will be a thing; target for software was unclear (to me).
