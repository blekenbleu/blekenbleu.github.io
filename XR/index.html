Issues (for me)
  • NOT nausea
  • without passthru:  frequent need to remove, reinstall HMD
  • prescription eyeglasses may not fit
  • switching between (misplaced?) eyeglasses and HMD with prescription inserts
  • VR hand controllers are aggravating, easily misplaced and unsuitable for virtual desktop or sims
  • custom objects (props) can be introduced to VR (e.g. games) by rendering STL without passthru cameras
  • passthru cameras are wanted for real time random objects in games or virtual objects (desktops) into real space
  • ability to generate 3D movies using binocular passthru cameras will be a bonus
