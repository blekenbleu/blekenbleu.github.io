Virtual Reality

Xtended /Mixed Reality
Some physical objects rendered in virtual space
or some virtual objects superimposed on real space rendition (3D video).

HMD nausea

  • visual motion illusion without physical cues
  • latency / refresh rate(?)
    few complain of nausea from 24fps film (72fps shutter)
  • Vergence-accommodation conflict

VR HMD Issues (for me)

  • NOT nausea
  • without passthru:  frequent need to remove, reinstall HMD
  • prescription eyeglasses may not fit
  • switching between (misplaced?) eyeglasses and HMD with prescription inserts
  • VR hand controllers are aggravating, easily misplaced and unsuitable for virtual desktop or sims
  • "real" objects (props) can be introduced to VR by STL descriptions without passthru cameras for e.g. games
  • passthru cameras are wanted for real time random objects in games or virtual objects (desktops) into real space
  • ability to generate 3D movies using binocular passthru cameras will be a bonus

HOTAS as VR controller

  • In Steam > Settings (SteamVR must be turned off) > Controller, General controller settings. Uncheck everything.
    Ensure that only Xbox Configuration Support and Xbox Extended Feature Support are enabled.
  • Go to detected controllers, customize layout of your HOTAS. Save.
  • Open SteamVR; a gamepad icon should appear.  if not, be sure to open SteamVR by clicking VR button in Steam.
  • Open Steam VR app and make applicable mappings from virtual gamepad.
