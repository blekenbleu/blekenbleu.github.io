Anti-malware
Antivirus software looks like a virus to other antivirus software
  • years ago, M$ Defender was not very good;  now, others waste resources
  • commercial software obfuscation to resist cracking can be exploited;
    much commercial software employs OEM obfuscation/installation utilities, which are targeted by hackers.
Modern malware is increasingly a web browser issue
Malwarebytes Browser Guard seems a worthwhile browser extension to block malicious websites
DuckDuckGo's Chrome Extension seems benign and low overhead..

Alleged advantages of paid over freeware utilities are mostly illusive.
CCleaner FREE can be configured for low overhead:
  • uncheck all but Add "Open CCleaner..." option to Recycle context menu in Options -> Basic settings
  • configure Cookies for minimal Cookies to keep
  • Disable Smart Cleaning
  • uncheck Updates options;  occasionally click Check for updates at lower right corner
  • Advanced options: 
  • uncheck all Privacy boxes
